Note Taking

Note-taking is the process of capturing and organizing important information for future reference. It improves comprehension, aids learning, and supports effective recall through methods like written notes, digital apps, or voice recordings.

Citation

Citation is crediting sources in academic work, maintaining integrity by following styles like APA or MLA to reference books, articles, and sources, preventing plagiarism.

Plagiarism

Plagiarism is using someone else's work without giving credit, a violation of academic and ethical standards that can lead to severe consequences.
