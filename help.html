Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I know if something is plagiarized?

    • Review the references and citations in the document.

    • Verify that they are accurate and correspond to the actual sources.

  • How to do proper note taking and citation?

    • Summarize key points in your own words, organize notes clearly, and credit sources with proper in-text citations and a formatted bibliography.
